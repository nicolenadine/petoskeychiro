Your First Chiropractic Visit

▼

Whether you're new to chiropractic care or returning after a break, we will walk you through every step. At your first chiropractic visit, we focus on understanding your health history, goals, and current concerns.

What to Expect:

  • One-on-one consultation with Dr. Mee-Lynn
  • Full evaluation and posture review
  • Gentle chiropractic adjustment (if appropriate)
  • Personalized plan for future care

Forms & Preparation

To make things easy, we'll send your intake forms via email before your visit. Please complete the forms ahead of time so we do not take time away from your visit.

What to Bring:

  • Any relevant medical information
  • Comfortable clothes that you can move in
  • A list of any current symptoms or goals

New to Massage? You're in the Right Place.

▼

Our massage sessions are custom-tailored to your body. Whether you're here for therapeutic or relaxation work, we'll make sure your visit feels safe, comfortable, and effective.

What You'll Get:

  • Warm, welcoming setup, optional water
  • Pressure adjusted to your comfort level
  • Recommendations for stretches or follow-up care if desired

Welcome to Active Release Technique

▼

Active Release Technique (ART) is a specific type of manual technique that allows for us to increase movement.

What You'll Get:

  • Movement evaluation: Please bring anything to show what causes the problem
  • Treatment through clothing
  • Recommendations for stretches or follow-up care if desired
